Essential Tools

You need all of these before Module 01

Claude Account

Sign up at claude.ai — the free plan works. Pro ($20/mo) gives you faster responses and more messages if you want it.

Website Hosting

Where your site lives. SiteGround or Bluehost are solid picks for WordPress. Squarespace, Wix, and Showit include hosting.

Domain Name

Your URL (e.g. yourbusiness.com). Buy through your hosting provider or Namecheap. Make sure it's connected to your hosting.

Website Platform Installed

WordPress + Elementor is recommended. Also works with Squarespace, Showit, Webflow, Framer, Wix. Just make sure it's installed and you can log in.

At Least One Good Photo of You

A professional headshot or high-quality candid. Goes on your homepage and about page. Phone photos are fine if the lighting is good.

A Few Focused Hours

Each module takes 1-2 hours. Block real time for it — your answers shape everything that gets built.
